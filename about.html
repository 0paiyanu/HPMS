About H.P.M.C

Welcome to the His Place Model College (HPMC), where education is not just a process but a journey of discovery and growth. At HPMC, we pride ourselves on providing a nurturing and intellectually stimulating environment for students to thrive.

Our institution is staffed with highly trained and qualified teachers who bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classrooms. Each faculty member is not only dedicated to teaching but is also actively engaged in cutting-edge research, ensuring that our students receive the latest and most relevant information in their respective fields.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond traditional teaching methods. HPMC is equipped with state-of-the-art teaching aids and resources to enhance the learning experience. From interactive technology in classrooms to well-equipped laboratories and libraries, we provide students with the tools they need to explore, experiment, and broaden their understanding of the world.

The curriculum at HPMC is designed to be dynamic and adaptable, encouraging critical thinking, creativity, and a passion for lifelong learning. We understand that every child is unique, and our teachers are adept at tailoring their approaches to meet the diverse needs of our students.

Join us at HPMC, where education is not just a destination but a continuous journey of growth, discovery, and empowerment. Your child's future begins here.
